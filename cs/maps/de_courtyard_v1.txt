Terrorists:
-Bomb the town bandstand or houses 
at ct spawn.

Counter-Terrorists:
-Prevent the Terrorists from destroying
the town centre.

Storyline:
During the previous week the towns people
caught the leader of a notorious terrorist 
faction living in their town. He was 
handed over to the regional police and
sentenced to life imprisonment. Now the 
terriorist group want revenge on the 
meddling towns people by destroying the 
historical 18th century bandstand that is the 
proud landmark of their town.
